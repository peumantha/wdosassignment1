Eco Products is a privately owned Sri Lankan manufacture since 2001(Member of the National Crafts Council), specialized in making environment friendly promotional items. Most products are hand-made according to customers' requirements, with customer specified logos and captions.

All products are Eco friendly & biodegradable and manufactured under Highest Quality Standards!

Our product portfolio itself has evolved to cover a wide range of value added products using multiple mediums from paper to fabric to wood. We source a range of embellishments and outsource a variety of handcrafted work to small suppliers with final assembly,

In 1993 a brief encounter with a piece of ‘recycled paper’ led us on a journey that would give rise to what was at that time a whole new industry in the country. The paper originated in a factory in Pannala, one of only two in the country that was making recycled paper.
